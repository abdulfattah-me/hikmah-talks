The Problem

Talent without a platform

Many young Muslims possess knowledge, passion and talent, but lack the confidence and communication skills to express themselves effectively — and the stage to be heard.

The Solution

An end-to-end pathway

HIKMAH Talks builds confidence, communication and leadership, then provides real opportunities to share meaningful stories that positively influence society.

Roadmap

The MVP, in three phases

Phase 1

Build

  • Brand identity
  • Landing page
  • Social media
  • Introductory workshop
  • Speaker application form
  • Mentoring framework

Phase 2

Develop

  • Pilot cohort of 10–20 participants
  • 4–6 training sessions
  • Coaching
  • Peer feedback
  • Rehearsals
  • Talk development

Phase 3

Amplify

  • Inaugural HIKMAH Talks event
  • 6–8 speakers
  • Professionally recorded
  • Published online
